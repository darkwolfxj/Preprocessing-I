Alastor

Intro

Alastor is the infamous entity known as "The Radio Demon", one of the many powerful Overlords of Hell. He is a main character in the cartoon, "Hazbin Hotel". He is known for making deals.

Skills

Alastor's main skills include smiling all of the time, commanding multidimensional forces that can bend anyone and anything to his will and being very scary when he wants to be. He is widely feared and respected throughout Hell and elsewhere.

Work History

During his lifetime, Alastor was a radio broadcaster. In the afterlife, he serves as an overlord of Hell.

Contacting Alastor

Currently, the only way to contact Alastor is to die and go to the same place as him and he will find you if he's interested in making a deal.
